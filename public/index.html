To all HF customers,

Thank you for your continued support during these trying times!


As of November 2020, we are not open for Indoor dining due to an order issued by the Governor of Illinois. We continue to be open for outdoor dining in our heated (and ventilated) Tent. We are also fully engaged in Takeout and Delivery. Our menu is available below at the bottom of the website and also by clicking on the Orange Toast button on our landing page (bottom left). You can order directly through this Toast button, pay, and we can deliver or you can pick up. Simply call us on 847 720 4466 when you arrive and we will run the food out to you!


We continue to follow all updated CDC and State guidelines. Staff are checked daily for their health condition including temperatures. All Staff wear masks/face coverings. Hands are washed frequently and sanitation stations are located throughout the restaurant. All utensils are sanitised after use. All menus are disposable/one-use. We are serious about prevention and thank you for helping us by also practising safe measures.

Here are some additional useful points:

  1. To order call 847 720 4466 or order from the Orange Toast button on our website, theharpandfiddle.com
  2. Order through GrubHub (and soon Uber Eats and Doordash)
  3. We will keep your food warm for you. When you arrive just call 847 720 4466 and we will bring your food to the front or to the Alley directly behind us.
  4. Check out our range of "Dinners for Two" and "Family Dinners" on our menu below. You can order for yourself or a neighborhood couple!
  5. Delivery is contactless. we will drop your food at the door and ring the bell and await your answering before departing, all the time keeping a safe distance.
  6. We delivery Wine, beer (Growlers/Howlers, Cans, bottles) and liquor. Please add to your Dinner order and we will bring directly to you.
  7. Hours are currently 11am to 8PM Sunday to Thursday and 11am to 9PM Friday and Saturday.

Thank you again!
